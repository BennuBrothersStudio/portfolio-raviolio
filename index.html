Featured Project

STICKMAN
BATTLEGROUND

Card Game System Design Original Art Kickstarter Age 14

A fully original card-based combat RPG — designed, illustrated, and written entirely from scratch in 9th grade. Characters have interlocking abilities, status effects, and enough mechanical depth to reward real strategy.

The game features characters like The Fancy Man ("Fancy Man is my name, Duplication is my game!"), The Supporter ("For legal reasons I cannot tell you that I'm a doctor"), and Glob — a shapeshifting monster whose method is listed as "Horrifically (bad) Disguises."

Successfully crowdfunded on Kickstarter, Spring 2023!
